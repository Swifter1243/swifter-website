Beat Saber is a virtual reality game where I have created over 50 of my own levels for it's community.

In order to create these levels, I had to create my proprietary tool ReMapper. Additionally, I had to invest hundreds and hundreds of hours into learning new concepts as well as spend loads of time iterating and polishing from feedback.

We're looking at multiple years of a major learning curve here, so I won't cover each map and all it's challenges, but there's some writeups on the most significant moments like BRLCTAP and Extra Sensory II.
