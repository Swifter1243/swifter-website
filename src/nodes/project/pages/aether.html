Aether is a completely original virtual reality experience inside of Beat Games' Rhythm game Beat Saber.

An in-game playthrough of Aether.

Aether was conceptualized over the course of 3 years, and created over the course of 300 hours. Nearly every asset (shader, animation, material, prefab) was created from the ground up by me.

My work on Aether

❮ ❯

Every shader effect in the map was created from scratch, allowing for a unique utilization of the render pipeline rarely seen in the Beat Saber community.

Most shapes were created through math, allowing for a unique artstyle across many complicated effects.

The result was a dense and constantly changing set of visuals that would not be possible without careful engineering.

In order to animate the dense contents of the song, I needed to have an audio preview in-editor while working on the animation.

Not many good tools existed that allowed me to work like this, so I created a tool to do this myself.

This tool also included particle playback, which allowed me to have a true "what you see is what you get" workflow.

Due to the upload limitations of the Beat Saber custom level site BeatSaver, asset bundles for the experience had to be under 5mb.

Using my knowledge of Unity systems and shader effects, I was able to bring the final file size of the bundles down to only 2.7 mb.

Aether was met with incredible reception, many saying it's the best Beat Saber map of all time.
