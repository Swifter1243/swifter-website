ZERO Inc. is a game developed by me and my friends Evan and Josh in 48 hours for a game jam.

A full playthrough of ZERO Inc.


You are a process inside of a computer tasked with wiping a 2.15 GB drive manually. Use the space you cleared to install upgrades which help you clear the drive faster.

During development I was mostly in charge of front-end presentation and design.

This included formatting our internal upgrades, creating small hints in the UI, and creating the grid that the player interacts with.

Here's an example of the reload animation I made for the grid which I really enjoy watching.

To give the game a distinct aesthetic, I created the CRT post processing effect to make the game really feel like it was being played on an old computer.

The effect is composed of horizontal bloom, scanlines, and curve distortion which I wrote in raw HLSL.
