SYNERGY is a rhythm game developed solely by me. I created all of the assets, and wrote all of the code.

A recording of SYNERGY gameplay.


The premise is that you slice discs to the beat, and occassionally a second cursor will appear that you also need to use.

During the process of developing this game, I created a plan, prototyped, and logged my progress every day.

The game includes one built-in level called "influx". The song was created by my friend Kitefield, and the visuals and level were created by me.

The visuals I created for 'influx'.


The game also has a fully functioning feature-rich level editor which lets you share and edit levels with other users.

A handful of people have already made custom levels!
