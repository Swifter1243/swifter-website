BLOON MAN is a game developed by me and my friend Evan in 48 hours for a game jam.

A full playthrough of BLOON MAN.


BLOON MAN, a platformer where you navigate a dangerous bouncy castle as a man made of balloons. Avoid sharp, spiky obstacles to keep bloon-men such as yourself safe as you find your escape.

I was responsible for creating a lot of effects to make the game feel visually reactive, utilizing scripting and Unity's particle systems.

Some examples of effects I worked on are door locking sequences, lock breaking effects, projectile impact particles, and level transitions.

We used prefab variants with Unity's Tilemap system to streamline level creation and experiment with what felt right with our mechanics.

I helped churn out about half of the levels in the project in order to get a respectable amount of content during the time limit.

I also created original music for the game.

I wanted to go for something sort of whimsical and triumphant, and I think it turned out pretty fun.

It won first place in the game jam at my college.
