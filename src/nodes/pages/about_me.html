Hey, I'm Elijah. I also go by "Swifter".

I am a programmer/artist who has been creating online since 2015. I'm known for creating visually striking and polished experiences that also require careful engineering.

While I have been creating, I've established myself as a notable figure in a handful of communities. Namely the Beat Saber community, where I have become one of the most popular level creators/modders that have pushed the game to it's limits.

Somewhere Out There which was the most popular level for 3 years!

Extra Sensory II which was the biggest event in Beat Saber history.

I am driven by the deep desire to demystify the world around me, such that I know every step involved to make something function.

This extends all the way into the roots of my personality, and would annoy my parents considerably when I was little. From watching shows like "How It's Made", to reading books about what to do with no materials alone in a forest, to now building this very website from the ground up, I love to deeply understand how everything fits together.

This has driven me to multiple fields of computer science, such as gameplay programming, graphics programming, system architecture, and some audio design. I have a wide variety of skills which are all documented on this website.

I'm motivated by impact, and creating experiences that will stick with the audience.

What drives me is creating magic for viewers that will change them a little bit after experiencing it, like some media has done for me.
